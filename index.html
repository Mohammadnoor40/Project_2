Joel Miller

Joel Miller is portrayed as a complex character with a troubled past, and his actions and decisions are central to the narrative of both games. The storytelling in "The Last of Us" series is known for its emotional depth and character development

Ellie

Ellie's character in greater depth, examining the emotional and psychological toll of living in a harsh and unforgiving world. The narrative follows Ellie's quest for revenge and the consequences of her actions

Abby

Abby is initially introduced as a member of the WLF (Washington Liberation Front), a militia group that operates in the post-apocalyptic world of "The Last of Us." Her storyline intersects with Ellie's, and players get to experience the events from both characters' perspectives

Bill

Bill is a secondary character whom Joel and Ellie encounter during their journey. He is a skilled mechanic and has set up a fortified town, but he is also a loner with a somewhat gruff personality. Joel and Ellie seek his help in repairing a vehicle

Tommy

Tommy is Joel's younger brother in the first game. He plays a significant role early in the story, but the relationship between Joel and Tommy becomes strained due to the harsh realities of the post-apocalyptic world.

Henry

Henry is introduced as part of a pair with his younger brother, Sam. They encounter Joel and Ellie during their journey through the post-apocalyptic world. Henry and Sam are initially cautious but later decide to join forces with Joel and Ellie in their common goal for survival
